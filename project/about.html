About Us

General Knowledge

GKToday (www.gktoday.in) is a leading website providing valuable, convenient, relevant and enjoyable online education pertaining to GK & Current Affairs to students, aspirants and general public through a diversity of services. This website is a venture of Suresh Soni, a former aspirant of Civil Services Examination. The main orientation of this website is to create a large repository of knowledge for the general reference of students and aspirants. GKToday was launched on December 28, 2008 and is a pioneer in providing high quality content to students and aspirants of various examinations in India. The 6 member team of GKToday includes former aspirants of the Civil Services Examination. How GKToday can help me in my preparation? GKToday covers the current affairs and conventional subjects. We update the current affairs quiz for SSC , Bank PO exams and other examinations. We post the articles related to entire gamut of General Studies to cater the needs of all other examinations. Bank PO Study Material provided on this site is a complete guide for you to understand the basics of the Banking in India. How GKToday helps in UPSC Civil Services or State Civil Services Exams? If you are preparing for Civil Services Exam, all the content of this e-magazine is important as a supplement to your studies. This site intends to cover current events in a systematic and time bound manner along with conventional subjects. GKToday also conducts Target Programmes comprising study material and mock tests to tackle the General Studies Papers of Civil Services Examination.
